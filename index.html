About Us

We wish to solve for systems ('X') in the H2X equation. We focus on solutions with innovative algorithms and seamless cyber-physical-human platforms. We are interested in advancing the state-of-the-art of systems that can efficiently understand and adaptively interact with humans in their environments. As a result, our research spans multiple areas of real-world system design, from machine perception to decision-making and human-machine interaction. Our overarching goal is to develop intelligent technologies with robust autonomy and real-time assistance capabilities.
